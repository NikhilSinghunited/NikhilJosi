Shiv Mohan Band, Since 1963

A Glimpse

A Glimpse Starting in the year 1963, Shiv Mohan Band is the very first nationally acclaimed brand in Delhi-NCR, and has been regarded as among the best marriage band in Delhi for decades. With 60 years of legacy, we have performed at a huge number of weddings, festivals, and high-profile events, and also for a function band. Our band consists of more than 300 band members from a number of musical backgrounds, playing a range of instruments like shehnai, nagada, clarinet, trombones, and trumpets. Many acclaim us for being the best band in Delhi.
